# Inbox

Raw notes from the user go here before they are normalized into tickets, decisions, designs, or wiki pages.

## Usage

- Keep this messy.
- Use bullets, pasted notes, questions, screenshots, or rough requirements.
- Codex should periodically convert inbox items into `contexts/tickets/`, `contexts/decisions.md`, `contexts/designs/`, or `contexts/wiki/`.
